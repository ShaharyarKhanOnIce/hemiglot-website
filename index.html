1. Effective language learning

Language learning apps either gamify too much, to the point that learning is ineffective, or are too boring.

2. Free language learning

Learning should be as free and as accessible as possible. There should'nt be a paywall to the most essential features.

3. Not annnoying

Free learning should only be substituted with a reasonable number of advertisements. Not an ad every second. Users should'nt also find something paywalled or too 'annoyified' that was marketed as free.

4. No AI

Content should be authentic. Langauges are a very human construct. Source material or lessons should'nt be created with AI.
