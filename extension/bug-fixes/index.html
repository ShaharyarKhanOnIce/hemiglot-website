Solutions for common bugs in the extension

You may have encountered some bugs, so here are some common solutions.

Potential Errors:

  1. Failed to Export
  2. No receiving end
  3. Not working on Duolingo
  4. Exporting takes a long time
  5. Words list is not downloaded

Proven Fixes:

Try these solutions based on the priority of their order

  • Make sure your internet connection is stable.
  • Make sure you are in the words tab on Duolingo.
  • If you are on the words tab and the extension does not work, reload the page. This occurs usually when the extension has just been updated.
  • Exporting the words takes a couple of seconds but if it takes longer, you may have lost your internet connection while the extension was reading them.
  • If your words are not being downloaded, review your permissions from your browser settings. The extension needs permissions to access your tabs and storage.
  • Try resetting your cookies and preferences history and reinstalling the extension.
